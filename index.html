About the Symposium

NEXORA'25 is an intra-department symposium designed to ignite innovation, creativity, and competitive spirit among students. Dive into a dynamic arena of challenges, workshops, and cultural showcases that foster collaboration, critical thinking, and excellence. From technical expertise to artistic brilliance, NEXORA'25 is the ultimate stage to learn, compete, and thrive. Be part of an experience where ideas meet action and talents shine the brightest!
